About Pat

Pat's was founded in 1985 in Seattle WA. For years, Pat has wanted to combine two of his favorite things Salmon and cookies. Since that fateful day Pat's world famous salmon cookies were born. There are over 50 flavors of these cookies. As of 2019, there over 5 city's globally that have a Pat's store front. As of this year, we have implemented a cookie counter at all of our stores around the world to show how much cookies we make on a normal day.
